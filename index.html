About Me

Hi, I’m Kayra. I’m a filmmaker. a writer. a content creator. an academic. a designer. a pianist. an activist. a barista. a programmer.

Whether I’m making films, writing stories, diving into research, or designing something new, I’m driven by the excitement of blending creative expression with analytical thinking. I love figuring things out, connecting unexpected ideas, and seeing where that journey takes me. I’ve never quite fit into the mainstream, and that’s where I feel most at home, where I can explore ideas that challenge the norm.

At heart, I’m a storyteller, no matter the medium. I believe in the power of unconventional ideas to spark change, or just make someone feel seen. I want to create work that resonates deeply and leaves a lasting impact. It’s why I find myself constantly bouncing between different fields, because each one offers a new way to express something meaningful. If I can make someone see things a little differently, especially those who feel they exist outside the box, then I’ve done what I set out to do.
